All the Things That Make Me...Me.
  • Sports

    • For as long as I can remember, I have been passionate about competing, and sports have provided me that outlet. For me, competition has never been solely about winning. When I compete, I feel energized and unstoppable. Competition is a fundamental part of who I am.

  • Books

    • Ever since ninth grade, reading has become one of my favorite hobbies. The book that sparked this passion was "Keepers of the Lost Cities." It was the first time I experienced a story so captivating that I couldn't put it down. I felt immersed in the narrative, compelled to discover what happened next. Since then, I have found books to be both comforting and a source of relaxation.
